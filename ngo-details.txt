Website Features:

1. Core Pages (The Foundation)
    Home/Landing Page: A high-impact Hero section with a clear mission statement, emotional imagery/video, and a "Donate" call-to-action (CTA).
    About Us: Mission & Vision: What drives the NGO.
    Our Story: How it started.
    Team/Board Members: Photos and bios to build trust.
    Financials/Transparency: Annual reports or simple breakdowns of where money goes (crucial for donor trust).
    Contact Us: Physical address, map, email, phone, and a contact form.
2. Action & Engagement (The "Engine")
    Donation System:
    Secure payment gateway integration (Stripe, PayPal, Razorpay, etc.).
    Options for One-time vs. Monthly giving.
    "Sponsor a Child/Project" specific options.
    Volunteer Hub:
    Information on how to help.
    Application form for volunteers.
    Get Involved: Corporate partnerships, fundraising ideas for supporters.
3. Impact & Content (The "Proof")
    Projects/Causes: Detailed pages for each major initiative (e.g., "Clean Water," "Education").
    Success Stories/Blog: Real stories of people helped. Updates on projects.
    Gallery: High-quality photos and videos of the work on the ground.
    Events Calendar: Upcoming fundraisers, webinars, or field visits.
4. Technical & Trust Features
    Newsletter Signup: To keep supporters engaged.
    Social Media Integration: Links to Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, etc.
    SEO Optimization: To help people find the cause.
    Mobile Responsiveness: Critical since most people browse on phones.

Proposed Admin Dashboard Features:


Dashboard Overview (Home)
    Quick Stats: Total donations this month, new volunteers, active projects.
    Recent Activity: List of recent donations or form submissions.
Content Management (CMS)
    Manage Projects/Causes: Add new causes, update progress bars, upload photos, edit descriptions.
    Manage Events: Create and edit upcoming events.
Manage Blog/Stories: Publish success stories and news.
Donation Management
    Transaction History: View all donations (date, amount, donor name).
    Export Data: Download donor lists for tax receipts or newsletters (CSV/Excel).
Volunteer & User Management
    Volunteer Applications: View and approve/reject volunteer signups.
    Admin Users: Add other team members who can manage the dashboard.
Settings
    General Info: Update NGO address, phone, email, and social links.
    Payment Settings: Configure API keys for Stripe/PayPal.
